_Sketchy memory palace for Acute Appendicitis_

| No. | Visual Cue | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Balloons shaped like '20' and '30' | Peak incidence in 20s and 30s |
| 2 | Empty plate and belt migrating to right hip | Anorexia followed by periumbilical pain migrating to RIF |
| 3 | Clogged drain with stone and purple grapes | Obstruction via faecolith or lymphoid hyperplasia |
| 4 | Three ribbons meeting at clamped red hose | Appendix base at taeniae convergence; end artery risk |
| 5 | MANTRELS chalkboard with score 7 | Alvarado Score ≥7 suggests surgery |
| 6 | Pregnancy test and leaking salt shaker | DDx: Pregnancy (females) and ureteric colic |
| 7 | CT toaster and Ultrasound machine | Imaging: CT for adults, USG for children/pregnancy |
| 8 | Three-hole apron and 60-minute stopwatch | Laparoscopic appendicectomy; antibiotics &lt; 60 mins before incision |
| 9 | Brick-walled fridge with 6-8 week calendar | Abscess (interval surgery 6-8 weeks); pylephlebitis |
| 10 | Microscope and appendix in a jar | Histology: Rule out carcinoid or adenocarcinoma |
